About me

Hi, I’m Alinda Byamukama — a legal associate turned web developer focused on building tools that simplify processes and improve access to services.

My background in women’s rights advocacy taught me how to break down complex information and design for real-world impact.

Today, I’m applying those same principles to web development — designing clean user interfaces in Figma, building frontend features in React.js, and contributing backend services using Django and FastAPI.

I’m passionate about creating accessible, user-friendly applications that empower people to achieve their goals. When I’m not coding, you can find me reading about the latest tech trends, exploring new places, or enjoying a good cup of coffee.
